    Professional Summary

    Dr. Malik Saad Sultan is an accomplished Associate Principal Engineer at the Hong Kong Applied Science and Technology Research Institute (ASTRI), the region's leading R&D center. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science and over a decade of expertise in artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision, Dr. Sultan has led groundbreaking projects in biometric recognition, smart sensing technologies, and healthcare diagnostics.

    🎓 His academic journey began with a BSc from the Federal Urdu University, Pakistan, followed by a Master's in Robotic Vision from the Beijing Institute of Technology, China, and a Ph.D. in Medical Image Processing from the University of Porto, Portugal. This strong foundation has enabled him to bridge cutting-edge research with industrial applications, resulting in patented innovations and high-impact publications.

    🚀 As Projects Lead and Senior Researcher, Dr. Sultan has led the development of advanced AI systems in autonomous vehicles, privacy-preserving sensing and behavior understanding, and computer-aided diagnosis. His contributions have earned him several prestigious awards, including the 🏆 Silver Medal at the 50th International Exhibition of Inventions Geneva (2025), 🏅 ASTRI’s Outstanding Employee Award (2022), and 🤝 ASTRI’s Best Team Award (2020–2022). His 🎓 academic achievements include the FCT-MAPi Grant for his PhD (2014), the RHEUMUS Research Grant (2014), and the CSC Grant for his Master’s degree (2010). He also received the Excellent Student Award from the International Student Centre (1st Prize, 2013) and the Distinguished Student Award from Beijing Institute of Technology (1st Prize, 2013).

    🌐✨ Combining technical leadership with strategic vision, Dr. Sultan continues to drive AI solutions that tackle real-world challenges, solidifying his reputation as a key innovator in the field.
