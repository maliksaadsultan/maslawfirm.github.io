Punjab Revenue Authority (PRA)

Sales tax registration and return filing in Punjab

Provincial Tax

What is Punjab Revenue Authority?

The Punjab Revenue Authority (PRA) is the provincial tax authority responsible for collecting sales tax on services in Punjab, Pakistan.

PRA was established to efficiently collect and administer various provincial taxes including sales tax on services. All businesses providing taxable services in Punjab must register with PRA and file regular returns.

Unlike federal sales tax which applies to goods, PRA sales tax applies to various services specified in the Punjab Sales Tax on Services Act, 2012.

Taxable Services

Services Covered by PRA

Restaurants & Catering

Restaurants, food chains, caterers, and hospitality services.

Automobile Services

Vehicle mechanics, workshops, car washes, and auto repair services.

Beauty & Salons

Beauty salons, spas, hairdressers, and wellness centers.

Transport Services

Cargo handling, freight transport, and logistics services.

Advertising

Advertising agencies, media buying, and promotional services.

Fitness Centers

Gyms, fitness clubs, sports facilities, and training centers.

Registration

PRA E-Enrollment Process

Step 1: NTN First

Obtain your National Tax Number (NTN) from FBR before applying for PRA registration.

Step 2: PRA Portal

Visit the official PRA website and complete the e-enrollment application form.

Step 3: Documents

Upload required documents including NTN certificate, business address proof, and bank details.

Step 4: Verification

PRA verifies your application and issues the registration certificate.

Return Filing

PRA E-Filing Process

Monthly Returns

File PRA sales tax returns monthly by the 15th of the following month.

Tax Calculation

Calculate 16% sales tax on taxable services provided during the period.

Input Tax Credit

Adjust input tax paid on business expenses against output tax liability.

Payment

Pay net tax liability through PRA authorized banks or online payment systems.

Active Taxpayer

Active Taxpayer List (ATL)

PRA ATL Requirements

Being on the PRA Active Taxpayer List is essential for:

  • Participating in government tenders and contracts
  • Maintaining legal compliance for business operations
  • Avoiding penalties and legal consequences
  • Building credibility with clients and vendors
