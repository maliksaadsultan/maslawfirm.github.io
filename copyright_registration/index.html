Copyright Registration in Taxila, Pakistan

What is Copyright

Copyright protects original works of authorship, like music, poetry, movies, novels, songs, software, and art, both published and unpublished. However, it doesn't cover facts, systems, methods, or ideas.

In Pakistan, copyright law grants authors ownership of their creations. When you write a book or poem, you own it as the author. Copyright registration ensures your ownership is protected.

Official Copyright Registration System in Pakistan

Copyright is an intellectual property protection similar to patents and trademarks. In Taxila and across Pakistan, copyright registration follows the IPO Pakistan system and the Copyright Rules, 1967. Once registered, the applicant legally owns their creative work, such as music, books, or websites. Copying the original work is not allowed after registration IPO authorities in Pakistan. Without the author's permission, using copyrighted material is prohibited. Consent is needed to use published works, and the author can charge for its use. Copyright registration in Taxila and Pakistan safeguards inventors from infringement and prevents unauthorized use or copying.

Copyright, Trademark, and Patent comparison

Copyright registration in Pakistan with the IPO safeguards an author's original work, while a patent protects inventions. A trademark protects words, phrases, or designs that distinguish the source of goods from one party to another. A service mark differentiates the source of a service instead of goods.

Basic Requirements for Copyright Registration in Pakistan

Copyright registration ensures legal protection of goodwill and prevents unauthorized copying of an author's work in Taxila and throughout Pakistan.

Necessary Details of Copyright Applicants in Pakistan
  1. To file a copyright registration application in Pakistan, provide the applicant's name, address, and nationality. For a company or firm, include the incorporation and registration certificates. 
  2. Soft Copies and CDs: Applicant must provide a soft copy of their work in PDF, JPEG, JPG, or GIF format. For computer program copyright registration, CDs or DVDs of the programs must be provided.
  3. Power of Attorney: A Power of Attorney, duly signed by the Applicant, authorizing the firm or advocate is required before filing a Copyright Registration Application in Taxila and other cities of Pakistan.
Documents Required for Copyright Registration in Pakistan
  • Name, Address, Business Name and Nationality of the Applicant – Copy of CNIC
  • Obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the publisher if the work is already published and the applicant for Copyright Registration is not the publisher.
  • Search Report from Trade Mark Office if any
  • obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the person whose photographs appear on the artistic work
  • Power of Attorney in favor of authorized Firm or Advocate signed by Applicant
  • 4 Copies of work
  • Pay order or Demand Draft in the name of Director General IPO Pakistan 
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the author if the applicant is not the author.

Procedure for How to Register Copyright in Pakistan
  1. An application, including all required details and copyright fees, should be sent to the IPO registrar. Each application must be signed by the applicant and an advocate with a Power of Attorney. Separate applications are needed for multiple works.
  2. If no objections are received within 30 days, the Examiner will check the Application for discrepancies. If none are found, the registration will be accepted and sent to the Registrar for entry in the Register and issuance of a Copyright Certificate.
  3. The Registrar will issue a Dairy No. and publish a Notice of Copyright in the newspaper. After publication, there is a mandatory waiting period for objections.
  4. If an objection is received, the Registrar notifies both parties and offers them a chance for a hearing.
  5. After the hearing, if both parties agree and resolve objections, the Registrar will decide to approve or reject the Copyright Application.
