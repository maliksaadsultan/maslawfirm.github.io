Real Estate & Construction Company Registration

Start your real estate or construction business with proper licensing

Business Setup

Register Your Construction Company

Real estate and construction companies in Pakistan require registration with multiple regulatory bodies including SECP, FBR, and Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC).

The construction and real estate sector in Pakistan is rapidly growing, especially in areas like Taxila with ongoing development projects. To operate legally, construction companies must obtain proper registrations and licenses.

We provide comprehensive assistance for construction company registration, ensuring compliance with all regulatory requirements including PEC certification for engineering firms.

Required Registrations

Regulatory Requirements

SECP Registration

Register your company with Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan under the Companies Act, 2017.

PEC Registration

Pakistan Engineering Council registration for construction and engineering firms to operate legally.

NTN Registration

National Tax Number from FBR for income tax compliance and filing.

Sales Tax Registration

Register for sales tax on construction services and property transactions.

LDA/Tehsildar Approval

Obtain necessary approvals from local development authorities for construction projects.

Trademark Protection

Protect your company brand with trademark registration at IPO Pakistan.
