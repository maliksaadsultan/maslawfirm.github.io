PUNJAB REVENUE AUTHORITY | SALES TAX ON SERVICES

The Punjab Revenue Authority is the legal entity responsible for managing the Sales Tax on Services in Punjab. This tax is an indirect levy applied to services listed in Schedule II as "taxable services." The provider of these taxable services must pay the service tax to the Punjab Revenue Authority.
