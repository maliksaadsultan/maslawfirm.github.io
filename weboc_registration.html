Weboc Registration in Taxila and around Pakistan provides a chance to traders availing different benefits offered by the GOVT of Pakistan in various import and export schemes. The process of Weboc registration in Taxila and around Pakistan is straightforward without future compliance and renewal. Weboc is a computerized system that provides custom clearance of import and export goods.

WEBOC Registration Process in Taxila, Pakistan

  1. Submission of application to Deputy/Assistant Collector WeBOC User-ID Section, along with supportive/required documents
  2. Personal appearance of the applicant before the Deputy / Assistant Collector User-ID Section with the original National Identity Card (NIC).
  3. The applicant will give Personal Appearance, and digital picture and thumb impression taken later on. 
  4. Visit of the Business premises by inspectors nominated by Deputy/Assistant Collector (wherever required)
  5. Acceptance/Rejection of Application.
  6. Creation of User-ID in case of acceptance of the application
  7. Issuance of Login-ID and sending a computer-generated password to the applicant through email.
