NTN Registration in Taxila

Get Your National Tax Number with Expert Guidance from MAS Law Firm

Tax Registration

What is NTN?

Every person applying for NTN registration in Taxila should follow the FBR's pattern. The Commissioner of Income Tax can register an NTN voluntarily based on the taxpayer's case. The individual's CNIC issued by NADRA will be used as the National Tax Number.

NTN Registration for Salaried Individuals

For the Individual Category, these documents are needed for NTN registration:

  • TRF-01 Form
  • Original CNIC
  • Mobile Number in the name of Applicant
  • Personal Email Address
  • Payslip / Employment Letter
  • Utility Bill paid (within the last three months from the application date)

NTN Registration for Business Individuals / Sole Proprietors

For business individuals and sole proprietors in Taxila, the following documents are required:

  • Taxpayer Registration Form (TRF-01)
  • Original CNIC
  • Mobile Number in the name of Applicant
  • Personal Email Address
  • Business Premises Ownership Documents / Tenancy Agreement along with Owner's CNIC Copy
  • Paid Utility Bill (within last three months from the date of Application)

NTN Registration for AOP / Partnership Firm / Trust / Foundation

For NTN registration of a Firm, AOP, or Trust, one partner must be present at the Income Tax Office with the following documents:

  • Registration Form (TRF-01)
  • Authority Letter for a Member/Partner to present documents in FBR on behalf of the Firm (signed and thumbed by all Members/Partners)
  • Original CNIC of Member/Partner
  • Email Address of AOP/Trust/Foundation
  • Mobile Number registered to Member/Partner, not previously used for NTN Registration
  • Business Premises Ownership Documents/Tenancy Agreement with Owner's CNIC Copy
  • Paid Utility Bill (from the last three months prior to application)
  • Original Partnership Deed/Trust Deed/Foundation MOA and AOA
  • Original Registration Certificate

NTN Registration for Companies

For NTN registration, one director must be present at the Income Tax Office with these documents:

  • Taxpayer Registration Form (TRF-01)
  • Authority letter authorizing the Director or CEO to present documents to the FBR on behalf of the company (signed and thumbed by all Directors/CEO)
  • Original CNIC of the Director/CEO
  • Company email address
  • Mobile number registered in the name of a Director/CEO, not previously used in NTN registration
  • Documents proving ownership or tenancy of business premises, along with the owner's CNIC copy
  • Utility bill paid within the last three months from the application date
  • Original incorporation certificate
  • Certified true copies of the Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA)
  • Form II (if registered under the Companies Act 2017)
