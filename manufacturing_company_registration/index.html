Manufacturing Company Registration

Start your manufacturing business in Pakistan with proper legal registration

Business Setup

Register Your Manufacturing Company

Manufacturing companies in Pakistan require proper registration with SECP, FBR, and relevant provincial authorities. MAS Law Firm provides comprehensive assistance.

Manufacturing businesses in Taxila and across Pakistan must register with multiple government bodies to operate legally. This includes company incorporation with SECP, tax registrations with FBR, and provincial tax authority registrations.

We help you navigate the complete registration process, ensuring all required licenses and registrations are obtained efficiently for your manufacturing operations.

Required Registrations

Required Registrations for Manufacturing

SECP Registration

Register your company with Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan under the Companies Act, 2017.

NTN Registration

Obtain National Tax Number from FBR for income tax purposes and compliance.

Sales Tax Registration

Register for sales tax if your annual turnover exceeds the prescribed threshold.

WEBOC Registration

Register with WEBOC for import of raw materials and export of finished goods.

PRA Registration

Punjab Revenue Authority registration for provincial tax compliance.

Trademark Registration

Protect your brand name and logo with trademark registration at IPO Pakistan.

Process Steps

Registration Procedure

Step 1: Name Reservation

Reserve your company name through SECP portal ensuring it complies with naming guidelines.

Step 2: Company Incorporation

File incorporation documents with SECP including memorandum and articles of association.

Step 3: Tax Registrations

Obtain NTN from FBR and complete sales tax registration processes.

Step 4: WEBOC Setup

Complete WEBOC registration for international trade operations.

Compliance

Ongoing Requirements

Annual Compliance

  • File annual returns with SECP
  • Prepare and file annual financial statements
  • Conduct mandatory annual audit
  • File income tax returns annually
  • File monthly/quarterly sales tax returns
  • Maintain proper books of accounts
