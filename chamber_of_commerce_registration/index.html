Chamber of Commerce Membership (LCCI)

Essential membership for businesses in Taxila and Rawalpindi region

Business Networking

Taxila Chamber of Commerce and Industry

The Taxila Chamber of Commerce and Industry (LCCI) serves thousands of registered businesses in the region, promoting economic growth and facilitating trade activities.

Why Join the Chamber?

The Taxila Chamber of Commerce and Industry plays a vital role in supporting local businesses. Membership provides access to networking events, trade fairs, training sessions, and connections with government bodies and international chambers.

Chamber membership is essential for obtaining Certificates of Origin, WEBOC registrations, and other import/export documentation required for doing business in Pakistan.

Membership Benefits

Why Business Membership Matters

Certificate of Origin

LCCI members receive attested Certificates of Origin required for international trade and export documentation.

WEBOC Registration

Chamber membership is mandatory for WEBOC (Web Based One Customs) registration, essential for import/export operations.

Business Networking

Connect with domestic and international business leaders, government officials, and trade delegations through Chamber events.

Trade Promotions

Access to international delegations, trade fairs, expos, and business matchmaking opportunities.

Research & Development

Chamber's R&D department provides business information, consultancy on trade issues, and market intelligence.

Government Liaison

Chamber bridges the gap between businesses and regulatory authorities, helping resolve trade-related issues.

Registration Department

LCCI Membership Services

New Membership

Processing of new membership applications for businesses seeking to join the Taxila Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

Membership Renewal

Annual renewal of existing membership with updated business documentation and fee processing.

Member Card Issuance

Issuance of official LCCI membership cards to registered members for identification and verification.

Document Attestation

Attestation of Certificates of Origin and commercial documents for international trade purposes.

Visa Recommendations

Issuance of visa recommendation letters and visa invitation letters for business travel abroad.

Annual Elections

Conduction of annual elections for LCCI managing committee positions among registered members.

Requirements

Documents for LCCI Membership

For New Membership Application

  • CNIC copy of proprietor/partners/directors
  • NTN (National Tax Number) in business name
  • Sales Tax Registration (if applicable)
  • Business Address Proof (utility bill or rental agreement)
  • Photograph of business premises
  • Bank certificate or account maintenance letter
  • Registration certificate (SECP/partnership deed if applicable)
Membership Objectives

LCCI Core Functions

Business Support

Supporting professionals and businesses through advisory services, skill development, and capacity building programs.

Regulatory Liaison

Effectively connecting the business community with regulatory bodies, government departments, and trade authorities.

Trade Relations

Establishing lasting business relationships with registered members through regular interactions and events.

Standards & Ethics

Maintaining high professional standards and promoting ethical trade practices within Pakistan's business community.
