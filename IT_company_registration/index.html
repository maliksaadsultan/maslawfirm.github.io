Register your technology business in Pakistan with PSEB certification

Technology Sector

PSEB Registration for IT Companies

Pakistan Software Export Board (PSEB) registration provides numerous benefits to IT companies and call centers operating in Pakistan.

The IT and call center industry in Pakistan has seen tremendous growth, with Taxila emerging as a hub for technology businesses. PSEB registration is essential for IT companies seeking to export software services and receive foreign exchange earnings.

We assist IT companies and call centers in obtaining PSEB registration along with company incorporation, tax registrations, and compliance requirements.

PSEB Benefits

Why PSEB Registration Matters

Foreign Exchange Earnings

PSEB registered companies can open foreign currency accounts and receive international payments directly.

Office Facilities

Access to PSEB incubation centers and office facilities at subsidized rates.

Training Support

Benefit from PSEB training programs and skill development initiatives.

Business Networking

Connect with international buyers and participate in trade delegations.

Tax Incentives

Access to tax holidays and rebates available for IT export companies.

Visa Facilitation

Expedited visa processing for business travel and client meetings abroad.

Registration Types

IT Business Categories

Software Development

Companies engaged in software development, customization, and maintenance services for international clients.

Call Centers/BPO

Business process outsourcing companies providing customer support, telemarketing, and back-office services.

Cloud Services

Companies providing cloud computing, hosting, and SaaS solutions to clients worldwide.

App Development

Mobile application development companies creating iOS, Android, and cross-platform applications.
