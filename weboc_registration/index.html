WEBOC Registration in Pakistan

Get your WEBOC ID for import and export operations across all Pakistani terminals

Import & Export

What is WEBOC?

WEBOC, developed by Pakistan Customs and PRAL, operates at terminals across Pakistan, including Taxila. Registration in Taxila and nationwide lets traders access government benefits for import and export schemes. The registration process is simple, with no recurring compliance or renewal requirement. WEBOC is a computerized system for customs clearance of goods.

Why Register for WEBOC?

To import or export goods from Pakistan, WEBOC registration is mandatory. Without it, you cannot proceed. Every business, firm, or company must follow a defined procedure for import and export operations.

Registration Process

7-Step WEBOC Registration Process

1

Submit Application

Submit the application to the Deputy/Assistant Collector WEBOC User-ID Section with the required documents.

2

Personal Appearance

The applicant must appear in person before the Deputy/Assistant Collector User-ID Section with the original National Identity Card (NIC).

3

Biometric Verification

The applicant will provide a personal appearance, with a digital picture and thumb impression taken later.

4

Premises Inspection

Inspection of business premises by inspectors appointed by the Deputy/Assistant Collector (as needed).

5

Acceptance/Rejection

Application is reviewed and either accepted or rejected with reasons provided.

6

User-ID Creation

Upon application approval, User-ID is created for the applicant.

7

Login Credentials

Issuance of Login-ID and email delivery of a computer-generated password to the applicant.

Registration Types

Types of WEBOC Registration

WEBOC Registration in Pakistan is issued based on the following suitability criteria:

Trader

  • Commercial (Manufacturer, Manufacturer-cum-Exporter, Commercial Importer, Commercial Exporter)
  • Non-Commercial
  • Ship-Breakers
  • Trust / Non-profitable Organization
  • Diplomatic Cargo (Embassies)
  • Government Department
  • Courier Services

Other Categories

  • Customs Agent
  • Bank Users
  • Bonded Carriers
  • Shipping Lines
  • Warehouse
